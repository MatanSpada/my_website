# Art direction

The exterior is a grounded mountainside threshold: layered rock masses rise above and around the arch, embedding the oak-and-iron door in a darker cave mouth. Brighter cool ambient light and a restrained warm guide retain mystery while making the path and silhouette readable. Sparse moss-green grass tufts soften nearby stone without turning the entrance into a forest. Texture richness, authored terrain, and final polish remain reserved for CAVE-006.
